# Intelligence Digest Design Specification

## Project Name: Prophet Code - The Bible Prophesy Dossier

## Project Goal
To create a comprehensive intelligence digest for an audience monitoring the return of Christ, integrating historical context, modern interpretations, and prophetic anticipation.

## Architecture: Chronological Flow (Recommended)
The digest will follow a linear progression to build understanding from foundation to future anticipation.

### Section 1: Historical & Foundational Context (The Roots)
*   **Purpose:** Establish the bedrock of belief, drawing from scripture and historical precedents.
*   **Content Focus:** Key scriptural passages, foundational doctrines, and relevant historical events/figures that serve as parallels or foreshadowing.
*   **Design Note:** This section must be dense with cited references and established context to ground the audience.

### Section 2: Modern Manifestations & Current Events (The Echoes)
*   **Purpose:** Connect the foundational truths to the present day. This section bridges the gap between ancient belief and contemporary reality.
*   **Content Focus:** How foundational concepts are currently being interpreted or manifested in modern society, media, or culture. Analysis of current events that align with or challenge these foundational beliefs.
*   **Design Note:** This section requires strong analytical skills to connect disparate pieces of information and demonstrate the ongoing dialogue between faith and the modern world.

### Section 3: Prophetic & Future Focus (The Horizon)
*   **Purpose:** Look forward, synthesizing the previous sections into anticipation of future events.
*   **Content Focus:** Synthesized analysis of signs, omens, or indicators pointing toward the return. Direct, focused anticipation of future events based on the preceding context.
*   **Design Note:** This section must be concise, impactful, and serve as the final, actionable takeaway or call-to-action.

## Key Principles
*   **Cohesion:** Ensure smooth transitions between sections, making the digest feel like a single, unified piece of intelligence.
*   **Depth vs. Breadth:** Balance the depth required in Section 1 with the breadth needed in Section 2, ensuring Section 3 remains focused and impactful.
*   **Clarity:** Ensure the language is accessible to the target audience while maintaining intellectual rigor.

---
**Commit Note:** This design outlines a linear structure for the intelligence digest, moving from historical foundations to modern application, and finally to prophetic anticipation.
