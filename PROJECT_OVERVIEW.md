# Prophet Code - The Bible Prophesy Dossier

## Project Vision
To create a comprehensive, high-end "Intelligence Digest" for an audience monitoring the return of Christ. The platform integrates historical context, modern interpretations, and prophetic anticipation into a single, unified experience.

## Core Goals
- **Educate:** Build a bedrock of understanding from historical scripture and parallels.
- **Connect:** Bridge ancient belief with contemporary reality through modern manifestations.
- **Anticipate:** Synthesize information into a focused, impactful look at future events.

## Target Audience
- Individuals seeking a rigorous, intellectually honest exploration of prophetic signs.
- Researchers and believers looking for a cohesive "intelligence" format rather than scattered content.

## Key Principles
- **Cohesion:** The digest must feel like a single, unified piece of intelligence.
- **Intellectual Rigor:** Maintain depth and clarity while ensuring the language is accessible.
- **Aesthetic Authority:** Use high-end typography and a "newspaper" aesthetic to convey weight and importance.
